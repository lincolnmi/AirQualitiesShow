组织架构

Steering Committee

Responsibility: 

  1. Institute annual program committee and appoint committee members 
  2. Define annual conference goal and mission 
  3. Define keynote speech and invited talks 
  4. Define session tracks

Annual Program Committee 

Responsibility: 

  1. Define the annual technical events 
  2. Review proposals and drive the selection process 
  3. Define regular webcast

CDUG Staff 

Responsibility: 

  1. Finance and logistics issues for annual events 
  2. CDUG website maintenance
