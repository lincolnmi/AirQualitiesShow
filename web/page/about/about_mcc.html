MCC Lab

The Mobile Cloud Computing Lab (MCC) in School of Software Engineering at Tongji University is a reserach team, which aims to conduct preliminary research on challenging problems in mobile cloud computing environments, involving large-scale mobile newtorks and individual mobile participatory sensing devices and technoloiges.

Tongji University, formerly Tongji German Medical School, was established in 1907. It was given its present name and became a state university in 1927. It was one of the oldest and most prestigious institutions of higher education in China.
